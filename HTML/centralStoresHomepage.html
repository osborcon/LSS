Logistics and Support Services (LSS) specializes in sales, logistics, asset management & warehousing.

LSS is comprised of Central Stores, Central Receiving, ISU Surplus & Material Stores

  • Need to purchase an item? Central Stores stocks over 6,000 SKU's. Not in stock? Central Stores can special order any item.

  • Expecting a freight shipment or need moving services? Central Receiving is ISU's logistics experts.

  • Looking to dispose or recycle an ISU asset? ISU Surplus redistributes and sells all ISU's retired assets.

  • Need to store items for future use? Warehousing has you covered.
